Senior Video Producer

Full-time

LOCATION

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (Office/Field Production)

About the Role:

AKAH Productions is seeking an experienced Senior Video Producer to lead our commercial and corporate video projects. You'll oversee productions from concept through delivery, ensuring creative excellence while managing budgets, timelines, and client relationships.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead end-to-end video production for commercial, corporate, and digital content
  • Develop creative concepts and treatments that align with client objectives
  • Manage pre-production planning including scripting, storyboarding, and shot lists
  • Direct on-set production, ensuring high-quality footage is captured efficiently
  • Oversee post-production workflow including editing, color grading, and sound design
  • Collaborate with our photography and social media teams for integrated campaigns
  • Maintain and operate professional 4K video equipment and lighting setups

Required Skills:

  • 5+ years professional video production experience
  • Expertise in cinematography, lighting, and audio capture
  • Proficiency with Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects
  • Strong portfolio showcasing commercial/corporate work
  • Excellent project management and client communication skills
  • Ability to work under tight deadlines in fast-paced environment
  • Knowledge of Saudi media landscape and cultural sensitivities

Why Join AKAH?

At AKAH, you'll work with Saudi Arabia's top brands on high-profile projects. We offer:

  • State-of-the-art production equipment and facilities
  • Opportunities for creative leadership and career growth
  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Health insurance and annual leave benefits
  • Continuous training on latest production techniques
  • Collaborative work environment with industry professionals
