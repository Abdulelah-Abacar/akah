Professional Background

Since founding AKAH in 2024, Khalid has led the company's creative vision, transforming it from a startup into one of Riyadh's most respected media production companies. Under his leadership, AKAH has delivered over 50 successful projects for regional and international clients, specializing in video production, photography, and digital content creation.

Khalid's approach combines technical precision with artistic storytelling, ensuring each project not only meets client objectives but also resonates with target audiences. When not on set, he mentors young filmmakers and photographers through AKAH's internship program.

Career Journey

Beginning his career at Saudi Broadcasting Corporation,
Khalid developed his production skills across various

Production Philosophy

At AKAH, we believe great production begins with understanding
the client's core message and target audience. Every frame we
capture serves the story we're telling, whether it's a brand
narrative, product showcase, or corporate message.

We combine Saudi cultural insights with international
production standards to create content that resonates locally
while maintaining global appeal. Our team stays at the
forefront of production technology while keeping storytelling
at the heart of everything we create.
